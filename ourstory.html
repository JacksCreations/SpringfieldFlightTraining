Who are we?

Central Illinois born and raised brothers Jack and Luke Lohmar are now available to help you achieve your aviation dreams!

We believe in more than just teaching the technical aspects of flying. We are dedicated to fostering a sense of camaraderie and a passion for aviation that goes beyond the cockpit. Our commitment to excellence, safety, and personalized instruction sets us apart, ensuring that every student receives the guidance and support needed to become a safe, confident, and competent pilot.

Whether you're a beginner with dreams of taking your first solo flight or an experienced pilot looking to enhance your skills, Springfield Flight Training is here to help you fulfill your aviation dreams!

What do we offer?

Training for...

  • Private Pilots
  • Instrument Rating
  • Proficiency flight training
  • Flight review
  • Instrument Proficiency Check (IPC)

Join us on this exciting journey as we transform aviation enthusiasts into skilled aviators!
