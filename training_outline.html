What aeronautical experience is required to become a private pilot? 61.109

Total Flight Time: At least 40 hours of flight time that includes at least:
  • Flight Training: At least 20 hours of flight training with an instructor, including:
    • 3 hours of cross-country flight training.
    • 3 hours of night flight training with 10 takeoffs and landings (with each involving a traffic pattern) and one cross-country flight over 100 nautical miles.
    • 3 hours of flight training on control and maneuvering of an airplane solely by reference to instruments.
    • 3 hours of flight training in preparation for the practical test within the preceding 2 calendar months.
  • Solo: At least 10 hours of solo flight time, including:
    • 5 hours of solo cross-country time.
    • 1 solo cross-country flight of at least 150 nautical miles total distance, with full-stop landings at three points and one segment of the flight consisting of a straight-line distance of at least 50 nautical miles between takeoff and landing locations.
    • 3 takeoffs and landings to a full stop at an airport with an operating control tower.
